Featured work

A coverage tool that catches denials before the claim goes out

Most coding tools check claims against Medicare national and local coverage rules alone, but a real book of business is mostly commercial, and every commercial payer publishes its own medical policy. I closed that gap by ingesting each payer's policies into a database of covered and non-covered procedure and diagnosis codes, then wiring it into a tool that gives the physician a real time coverage read at the point of order. Coverage decisions moved upstream to the moment of care, so the team stopped generating denials it could never win.
